What really an Internship is...?

  • Internship is an on the job training with defined time-frame which is aimed at giving a real time job experience to the Intern.
  • It gives an Intern the opportunity to start career in their chosen area of academics and interest.
  • Employer’s hand-hold the Interns, train, mentor them to understand various facets of the chosen area of Internship. They also assess the capabilities, potential, performance of Intern during the Internship period.

    • Benefits of Internship...

  • Gives you a start to your dream career.
  • Provides you required practical exposure, hands-on experience of the job.
  • Helps you gain insights of an organization, how they work and perform on regular basis.
  • Help inculcate the sense of professionalism and corporate readiness.
  • Build a network of Industry leaders, professionals to assist you with future career choices.
  • Learn, grow as an Industry professional.
  • Greater chances of turning to a paid role either with the same organization or otherwise.
